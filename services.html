Services

Smooth Surfaces & Stronger Streets

Revitalize Your Roads with The Experts

Sealcoating

The primary service, involving the application of a protective coating to asphalt pavements to shield them from the damaging effects of water, oils, and UV light.

Crack Filling

Sealing cracks in the pavement to prevent water from entering and causing further damage.

Pothole Repair

Filling and repairing potholes to restore the surface integrity and safety of the pavement.

Line Striping

Applying traffic paint for parking lots and roadways to clearly mark parking spaces and traffic lanes.

Asphalt Repair

Beyond potholes, this includes fixing larger areas of distress or deterioration in the asphalt.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular inspections and maintenance work to extend the life of the pavement.

Cleaning and Preparation

Ensuring the pavement surface is thoroughly cleaned before any sealcoating or repairs are done.

Traffic Control

Managing traffic during the maintenance projects to ensure safety for both the workers and pedestrians or drivers.

Consultation and Assessment

Providing assessments and recommendations for pavement maintenance plans based on the specific conditions and needs of the pavement area.

Clearing and Cleaning Parking Lots

Cleaning parking lots with various equipment to improve aesthetics and professionalism, and to enhance the likelihood of client visits.

Professional Bollard Installation Services

Enhance the safety and aesthetics of your property with our expert bollard installation services. Our team specializes in the strategic placement and secure installation of a variety of bollards tailored to meet your specific safety and design requirements.

Our Process

Our Process

Consultation and Site Assessment

We begin with a thorough assessment of your site to determine
the optimal placement and type of bollards that best suit your
needs. Whether it's for traffic control, security, or aesthetic purposes,
we ensure that our solutions meet your objectives.

Selection of Bollards

We offer a wide range of bollards, including fixed, removable, and retractable
options in various materials such as steel and concrete. Our team will help you choose
the right bollards that align with your security requirements and environmental conditions.

Precision Installation

Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art equipment to mark and excavate the designated areas, ensuring minimal disruption to your property. We handle all aspects of the installation, from preparing foundations to the placement and setting of bollards, ensuring a seamless and secure fit.

Finishing and Cleanup

After installation, we conduct a meticulous cleanup of the site and provide finishing touches to the bollards to enhance their appearance and durability. We also offer custom color options to match your landscape or branding.

Safety Inspection and Final Walk-Through

Post-installation, we perform a rigorous safety inspection to ensure that all bollards are installed correctly and function as intended. We provide a final walkthrough with our clients to guarantee satisfaction.
